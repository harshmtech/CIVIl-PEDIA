Civil Engineering

Civil engineering is a professional discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment.

History

Civil engineering applies physical and scientific principles to solve societal problems. Its history is closely linked with physics, mathematics, and other engineering fields.
